What is BMI?

BMI, or Body Mass Index, is a simple calculation using a person's height and weight. It's used to screen for weight categories that may lead to health problems.

Our BMI calculator allows you to quickly determine your BMI and see which category you fall into: underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese.

General Health Information

Maintaining a healthy weight is essential for overall health. Body Mass Index (BMI) is a useful indicator, but it's important to pair it with a balanced lifestyle. Explore the following health tips to help you stay on track.

BMI Categories

BMI Range Category
Less than 18.5 Underweight
18.5 - 24.9 Normal weight
25.0 - 29.9 Overweight
30.0 and above Obese

Health Tips for Maintaining a Healthy BMI

  • Eat a balanced diet: Focus on whole grains, fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and healthy fats.
  • Exercise regularly: Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate exercise, such as walking, five days a week.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink plenty of water throughout the day to stay hydrated.
  • Avoid processed foods: Minimize the consumption of sugary, fatty, and processed foods.
  • Get enough sleep: Ensure you get 7-9 hours of sleep per night to maintain optimal health.
  • Monitor your BMI: Regularly check your BMI to stay on track with your health goals.
